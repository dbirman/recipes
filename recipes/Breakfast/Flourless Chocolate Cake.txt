DIRECTIONS
----------
(See notes on Evernote)
1. Preheat oven to 300 F. Grease a 10 inch round cake pan.
2. In a small saucepan over medium heat dissolve the salt and sugar in the water.
3. In a double boiler (or in a microwave, stirring every 30 seconds) melt the chocolate.
4. Cut the butter into pieces and beat the butter into the chocolate, one piece at a time.
5. Beat in the hot sugar water.
6. Slowly beat in the eggs, one at a time.
7. Pour the batter into the prepared pan.
8. Bake in a hot water bath for 30-45 minutes. Remove from oven before center looks fully cooked.
9. Chill overnight. Dip cake pan in hot water to release cake before turning over on to a plate.  Return to refrigerator or freezer for an hour before dusting with powdered sugar, if desired.
#INGREDIENTS
120 Water
1.25 Salt
180 White sugar
510 Chocolate (bittersweet, semisweet or very dark)
240 Unsalted Butter
0 6 Eggs
