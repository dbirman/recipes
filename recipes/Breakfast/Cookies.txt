#TEXT
1. Preheat oven to 375
2. Combine the dry ingredients into a bowl
3. In a separate bowl mix the two sugars and butter
4. Once they are mixed put in the egg and vanilla and mix
5. Then gradually add the dry ingredients to the mix
6. Once well mixed add the chips
#INGREDIENTS
300 Flour
2.5 Salt
2.5 Baking Soda
120 Butter
60 Granulate Sugar
120 Brown Sugar
240 Choclate Chips
5 Vanilla
0 1 Large Egg
